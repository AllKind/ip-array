ecn: ecn match specification.
	The ECN target is also supported,
	but it only has one option without an argument.
	Thus the use of it implies the usage of the option.

	Synopsis: [!]option[:opt_arg][:...]
		options:
			[!]ip-ect 0-3
			[!]tcp-cwr
			[!]tcp-ece

	Examples:
		ecn=ip-ect:0
		ecn=!tcp-cwr:!tcp-ece
