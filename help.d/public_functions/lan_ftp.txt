
Name: lan_ftp

Description:
	Permit forwarded FTP traffic from certain LAN(s). - Requires `ALLOW_LAN_FTP' variable to be configured.
	Permit active FTP by setting the `ALLOW_ACTIVE_FTP' and 'ACTIVE_FTP_INTERFACES' variables.
	Permit passive FTP by setting the `ALLOW_PASSIVE_FTP' and 'PASSIVE_FTP_INTERFACES' variables.

Usage:
	lan_ftp
	
Example:
	lan_ftp
